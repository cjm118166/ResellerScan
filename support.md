## Subscription Management
If you need to cancel your ResellerScan Pro subscription or request a refund, please note that all billing is handled securely through Apple. 

To manage your active subscriptions:
1. Open the **Settings** app on your iPhone.
2. Tap your **Name/Apple ID** at the top.
3. Tap **Subscriptions**.
4. Select **ResellerScan** to manage or cancel your plan.
